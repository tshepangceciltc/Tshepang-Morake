I like Travelling

What makes me to travel:-

  • To see the beauty of cities.
  • learn different cultures.
  • I like travelling because it pulls me out of autopilot. New streets, languages, and food force me to pay attention and stay curious. I collect moments: sunrise over Table Mountain, late-night talks with strangers who become friends, markets that smell like spice and diesel. Travelling teaches me how small I am and how big the world is, which kills ego fast. It resets my stress, gives me stories worth telling, and reminds me that comfort isn’t the same as happiness. Every trip leaves me with sharper senses, looser plans, and a deeper appreciation for home when I return.

Benefits of travelling

  1. Meeting new people.
  2. Eating different type of foods.
  3. learning about each city laws.
